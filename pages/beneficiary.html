Our Mission

Every Why Racing event is partnered with one or more local beneficiaries. We provide a platform for non-profits to raise awareness and funds, ensuring that the energy generated on race day fuels positive change in our community long after the finish line.

Whether it's supporting adolescent mental health, feeding local families, or funding cancer research, your participation makes a direct impact.

Road + Trail: A Spectrum of Support

Road Racing

Our road events often benefit large-scale community initiatives, local school districts, and city-wide health programs. They bring thousands together in the heart of our community.

Trail & Nature

Our trail events help preserve local parks, support forest conservation, and fund outdoor youth education programs. We race in nature to protect nature.
