The Essentials

Everything you need to begin

01

Right Gear

You don't need expensive equipment. A supportive pair of running shoes and moisture-wicking socks are your best friends.

02

Consistency

It's not about speed; it's about showing up. Three days a week is plenty to build the foundation for your first 5K.

03

Hydration

Drink water throughout the day, not just during your runs. Your muscles need hydration to perform and recover.
