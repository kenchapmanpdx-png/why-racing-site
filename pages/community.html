Events We Support

Great races happen beyond our own calendar. These are community events we proudly cheer for and encourage our athletes to check out.

January 25, 2026

Race for the Warmth

1200 Fort Vancouver Way, Vancouver, WA
5K 10K Kids Race Fundraiser

Organized by Clark Public Utilities, this beloved community race raises money for Operation Warm Heart — helping limited-income families cover winter energy bills. A flat, fast course through Fort Vancouver and along the Columbia River.

Register
February 22, 2026

Vancouver Lake Half Marathon

Vancouver Lake Park, Vancouver, WA
Half Marathon 4-Mile Run Fast & Flat

A community favorite at Vancouver Lake Park — flat and fast along the lake and out to Frenchman's Bar. Capped at 475 entries and frequently sells out. Half starts at 10:00 AM, 4-Miler at 10:20 AM. Medals for all half finishers.
