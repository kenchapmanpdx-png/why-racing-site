ü¶∂ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this trail suitable for beginners and families?
Absolutely! The 5K course is mostly flat with well-maintained forest trails, perfect for first-timers and families. The 10K has some gentle hills for those seeking a bit more challenge. Strollers are welcome on the 5K course!
What should I wear?
Trail running shoes are recommended but not required. Bigfoot costumes and forest-themed attire are highly encouraged - we have prizes for best costume! Dress in layers as forest trails can be cool in the morning.
Will there really be Bigfoot sightings?
We can't make any promises... but Skamania County is officially designated Bigfoot refuge territory. Keep your cameras ready! üì∏ Our course does feature some "mysterious footprints" along the way.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds, but transfers are easy through your RunSignUp account.
Is the Kids Bigfoot Dash really free?
Yes! The Kids Bigfoot Dash (ages 12 and under) is completely free with any adult registration. Kids receive a participation ribbon and get to search for "Bigfoot clues" along a short, fun course.
