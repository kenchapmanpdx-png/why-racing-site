Frequently Asked Questions

What are the cutoff times for each distance?
50K: 11 hours (6:00 PM finish). 50 Mile: 16.5 hours (10:00 PM finish). 100K: 22 hours (2:00 AM next day). Intermediate cutoffs apply at aid stations‚Äîsee runner manual for details.
What mandatory gear is required?
All distances require: headlamp with spare batteries, emergency blanket, whistle, 1.5L hydration capacity, rain jacket, and warm layer. 100K runners also need drop bags at designated aid stations. Full list provided in runner manual.
Are pacers and crew allowed?
Crew access is available at designated aid stations only. Pacers are allowed for 50 Mile (after mile 30) and 100K (after 50K mark). Pacers must register in advance‚Äîincluded with registration.
What if there's snow on course?
We monitor conditions closely. Light snow/ice is expected‚Äîtrail spikes/microspikes recommended. In case of severe weather, we have backup course options and will communicate updates via email and social media.
What is the refund/deferral policy?
Deferrals to the following year are available until November 15 for a $25 fee. Transfers to another runner are allowed until December 1. No refunds available, but 100% deferral credit applies if race is cancelled due to conditions.
Is this race good for a first ultra?
The 50K is appropriate for experienced trail runners ready to step up. The terrain is challenging but the support is excellent. We recommend having completed a trail marathon or significant mountain running before attempting this course.
