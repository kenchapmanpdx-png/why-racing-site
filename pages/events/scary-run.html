Scare Yourself Silly

The Scary Run isn't just a race; it's a Halloween tradition! Join thousands of costumed runners for the PNW's spookiest and most fun running event. Whether you're racing for a PR or just enjoying the eerie atmosphere, this is an experience you won't forget.

The course winds through scenic Washougal with surprise scares, themed aid stations, and the best post-race party of the season. Costumes are encouraged (and judged for prizes!).
