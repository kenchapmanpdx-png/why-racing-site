Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes are the Santa suits?
Santa suits come in Adult S/M, Adult L/XL, and Kids sizes. Please select your size during registration. Suits run generous!
What age is the Kids Fun Run for?
The Kids Fun Run (1 mile) is perfect for children ages 12 and under. Parents are welcome to run alongside their little Santas!
What time does packet pickup open?
Packet pickup and Santa suit distribution is available the day before (December 4) from 3pm-7pm. Race morning pickup opens at 7:30 AM.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds.
What if it's cold or rainy?
The race goes on in all weather! We recommend layers under your Santa suit. Hot cocoa will be waiting at the finish line to warm you up!
