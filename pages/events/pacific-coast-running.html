Run Where the Ocean Meets the Sand

Experience the ultimate beach running adventure at the Pacific Coast Running Festival! Feel the ocean breeze, hear the waves crashing, and run along one of Washington's most stunning coastal stretches. Whether you're chasing a PR or simply soaking in the breathtaking views, this is the Pacific Northwest's premier beach running experience.

The course takes you along the beautiful Long Beach Peninsula, featuring firm packed sand, scenic ocean vistas, and the refreshing coastal atmosphere. Start your run at sunrise and finish to the sound of seagulls and the smell of salt air.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does packet pickup open?
Packet pickup is available the day before (Friday, September 11) from 2pm-7pm at the Long Beach Convention Center. Race morning pickup opens at 5:30 AM at the start line.
What type of surface is the course?
The course runs primarily on packed sand along the beach. We recommend trail or road running shoes with good traction. The sand is firm and well-maintained for optimal running conditions.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds, but transfers are easy through your RunSignUp account.
Is parking available at the beach?
Yes! Free parking is available at designated beach lots. We recommend arriving early as spots fill up quickly. Shuttle service is available from overflow parking areas.
What if there's bad weather?
The race runs rain or shine - it's the Pacific Coast after all! In case of dangerous conditions (lightning, extreme winds), we'll communicate updates via email and social media. Dress in layers and be prepared for coastal weather.
