Frequently Asked Questions

What time does packet pickup open?
Packet pickup is available the day before (Friday, March 13) from 3pm-7pm at the race venue. Race morning pickup opens at 6:30 AM.
Is the course certified?
The 10K course is USATF certified and qualifies for Boston Marathon qualifying times. The 5K and 15K are accurately measured but not certified.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds, but transfers are easy through your RunSignUp account.
Are strollers/dogs allowed?
Strollers are welcome! Dogs in green are allowed in the 5K only. Service animals are welcome in all distances.
Do I have to wear green?
Green attire is encouraged but not required. The more festive, the better! We have a Best Dressed contest with prizes for Most Spirited, Best Group, and Most Creative costumes.
