Frequently Asked Questions

What time does packet pickup open?
Packet pickup is available the day before (February 6) from 3pm-7pm at White River Sno-Park. Race morning pickup opens at 7:30 AM.
Are the snowshoes provided for everyone?
Yes! Quality snowshoes are included with every registration. Just show up and run.
What if there's no snow?
The race goes on in all conditions. If snow is insufficient for snowshoeing, the course will be adapted for trail running.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds.
What should I wear?
Dress in layers! Temperatures at the Sno-Park can vary. We recommend moisture-wicking base layers and a warm outer layer.
