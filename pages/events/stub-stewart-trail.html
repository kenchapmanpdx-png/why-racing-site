Frequently Asked Questions

What time does packet pickup open?
Packet pickup is available the day before (April 17) from 3pm-7pm at Stub Stewart State Park. Race morning pickup opens at 6:30 AM.
How difficult is the course?
The course features rolling terrain with moderate elevation gain. The 10K has about 500ft of gain, while the 25K has approximately 1,200ft. Suitable for intermediate trail runners.
Are trekking poles allowed?
Trekking poles are allowed in the 25K distance. They are not permitted in the 10K.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds.
Do I need a parking pass?
A valid Oregon State Parks day-use permit is required for parking. You can purchase one online or at the park entrance. Race registration does not include parking.
