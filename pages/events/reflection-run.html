Honor Those Who Served

The Reflection Run is more than a race - it's a meaningful way to honor those who served our country. Join us on Memorial Day weekend for a moving tribute that combines fitness with remembrance.

The course features memorial flags along the route, recognizing fallen heroes and Gold Star families. A portion of all proceeds goes directly to local veteran organizations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does packet pickup open?
Packet pickup is available the day before (May 22) from 3pm-7pm at the race venue. Race morning pickup opens at 6:30 AM.
Which organizations receive the proceeds?
Proceeds benefit local veteran organizations including the Clark County Veterans Assistance Center and local Gold Star family support programs.
Are there military discounts?
Yes! Active duty military, veterans, and their immediate family members receive 20% off registration. Use code VETERAN at checkout.
Is the course certified?
The half marathon course is USATF certified. The 5K and 10K are accurately measured but not certified.
Is there parking available?
Free parking is available at the race venue and surrounding areas. We recommend arriving by 7:00 AM for the opening ceremony.
