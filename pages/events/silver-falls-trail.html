Frequently Asked Questions

What time does packet pickup open?
Packet pickup is available the day before (March 13) from 3pm-7pm at Silver Falls State Park. Race morning pickup opens at 5:00 AM for 50K runners, 6:00 AM for others.
Is the course marked?
Yes, the course is fully marked with flagging, signs, and volunteers at key intersections. You won't get lost!
Are trekking poles allowed?
Trekking poles are allowed for the 50K distance only. They are not permitted in the 10K or half marathon.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds.
What about rain?
The race goes on rain or shine! Trails may be muddy - trail shoes with good traction are strongly recommended.
