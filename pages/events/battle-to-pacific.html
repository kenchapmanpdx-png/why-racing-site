Frequently Asked Questions

How does the relay work?
The 120-mile course is divided into 36 legs ranging from 2-7 miles each. Teams of 6 run 6 legs each; teams of 12 run 3 legs each. One runner runs at a time while the team follows in their vehicle(s) to the next exchange point.
What vehicles do we need?
6-person teams need 1 large vehicle (minivan or SUV recommended). 12-person teams typically use 2 vehicles split into Van 1 (runners 1-6) and Van 2 (runners 7-12). Vehicles must have working headlights and hazard lights.
What about overnight logistics?
Teams run through the night! Exchange points have bathrooms and basic facilities. We recommend bringing sleeping bags for runners to rest in the vehicle between legs. Hot coffee and food are available at major exchange points.
What safety gear is required?
All runners must have a reflective vest and headlamp/flashlight for nighttime legs (between 6pm-6am). A blinking tail light is also required. Safety vests are included in your registration!
Can we modify our team roster?
Yes! Team captains can modify their roster up to 48 hours before the event through their RunSignUp account. Runner substitutions are free and unlimited until then.
What if a team member can't run their leg?
Other team members can cover additional legs if needed. For 12-person teams, this is easier. We also have a "Lone Wolf" option where individuals can join teams needing extra runners.
