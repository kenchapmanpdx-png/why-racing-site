‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What time does packet pickup open?
Packet pickup is available the day before (April 24) from 3pm-7pm at Esther Short Park. Race morning pickup opens at 6:30 AM.
Is the course certified?
Yes! All distances are USATF certified and qualify for Boston Marathon qualifying times.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds.
Are strollers/dogs allowed?
Strollers are welcome in the 5K only. Dogs are not permitted on the course. Service animals are always welcome.
Is there parking available?
Free parking is available at Esther Short Park and surrounding downtown Vancouver lots. We recommend arriving by 7:00 AM.
