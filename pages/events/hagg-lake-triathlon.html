Frequently Asked Questions

What is the water temperature at Hagg Lake in June?
The water temperature typically ranges from 65-72¬∞F (18-22¬∞C) in mid-June. Wetsuits are permitted and recommended for athletes who prefer extra warmth. We'll provide a water temperature update the week of the race.
Is there parking at the venue?
Yes! Free parking is available at Scoggins Valley Park. The parking lot opens at 5:00 AM on race day. We recommend arriving early as this is a popular event. Carpooling is encouraged.
Where can spectators watch the race?
Spectators can watch the swim from the beach area, view transitions near the boat ramp, and cheer runners along the lakeside trail. The finish line area has excellent viewing and the post-race celebration zone.
What are the Sprint and Olympic distances?
Sprint: 750m swim, 20km bike, 5km run. Olympic: 1500m swim, 40km bike, 10km run. Both courses are fully marked and supported with aid stations throughout.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds, but transfers are easy through your registration account.
Is this race beginner-friendly?
Absolutely! Hagg Lake Triathlon welcomes first-time triathletes. The Sprint distance is perfect for beginners. We offer a pre-race clinic the day before and have excellent on-course support for athletes of all levels.
