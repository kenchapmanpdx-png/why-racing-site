Frequently Asked Questions üíú

Is this race only for women?
Yes! The Girlfriends Half Marathon is a women's-only event celebrating sisterhood and achievement. We welcome all women regardless of pace, age, or experience level.
What if I've never run a half marathon before?
This is the PERFECT first half marathon! Our course is designed to be beginner-friendly with generous time limits, enthusiastic volunteers, and aid stations every 1.5 miles. Many women complete their first half marathon with us!
Can I walk the course?
Absolutely! We welcome walkers and run/walkers. Our 4-hour time limit for the half marathon is walker-friendly. Many participants walk and enjoy every moment of the sisterhood experience.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers to another woman up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds, but transfers are easy through your RunSignUp account.
Is there packet pickup the day before?
Yes! Packet pickup is available Friday, September 25 from 2pm-7pm at the Expo. Race morning pickup opens at 6:00 AM. We recommend Friday pickup to avoid race morning lines!
