üèÖ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the course certified for Boston Qualifying?
Yes! Our marathon and half marathon courses are USATF certified and fully qualify for Boston Marathon registration. Your official time will be accepted by the BAA.
What BQ pace groups are available?
We offer pace groups for all Boston Qualifying times, from 3:00 to 4:55. Our experienced pacers are Boston Marathon veterans who know exactly how to help you achieve your goal.
How flat is the course really?
The marathon course has only 47 feet of total elevation gain - virtually pancake flat! The course follows the Columbia River corridor with gentle, almost imperceptible grades. It's one of the flattest certified marathons in the United States.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 30 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 14 days before. We want you to race when you're ready!
What are typical weather conditions?
Late August in Vancouver, WA typically features temperatures of 55-65¬∞F at the start, warming to 70-75¬∞F by finish. Overcast mornings are common, providing ideal racing conditions.
Is there on-course support?
Absolutely! Aid stations every 2 miles with water, electrolytes, and nutrition. Medical support, timing mats every 5K, and enthusiastic crowd support throughout the course.
