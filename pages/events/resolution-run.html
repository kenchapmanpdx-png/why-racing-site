Start Your Year Strong

Kick off 2026 with the Resolution Run! This beloved New Year's Day tradition brings together runners of all abilities for a refreshing start to the year. Whether you're chasing a PR or just shaking off the holiday celebrations, this is your race.

The flat, fast course winds through beautiful downtown Vancouver with stunning views of the Columbia River. After the race, enjoy our famous hot cocoa bar and celebration tent.
