‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What time does packet pickup open?
Packet pickup is available the day before (December 31) from 3pm-7pm at Esther Short Park. Race morning pickup opens at 7:30 AM.
Is the course certified?
The 10K course is USATF certified. The 5K is accurately measured but not certified.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 14 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 7 days before. No refunds, but transfers are easy through your RunSignUp account.
Are strollers/dogs allowed?
Strollers are welcome in the 5K! Dogs are not permitted due to course conditions. Service animals are always welcome.
What if it's raining?
The race goes on rain or shine! This is the Pacific Northwest after all. We recommend dressing in layers and bringing a change of clothes for after the race.
