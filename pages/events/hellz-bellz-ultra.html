üî• Frequently Asked Questions

What are the cutoff times?
50K runners have 10 hours from their start (4:00 PM cutoff). 50 Mile runners have 16 hours (8:00 PM cutoff). There are intermediate cutoffs at each major aid station posted on the course map.
How should I prepare for the heat?
Heat acclimation is critical. We recommend at least 2 weeks of heat training before the event. Start early, carry extra water, use electrolytes generously, and wear light colors. Temperatures can exceed 90¬∞F on exposed sections.
Are pacers and crew allowed?
Pacers are allowed for 50 Mile runners after the 30-mile mark. Crew access is available at 4 aid stations along the course. Detailed crew guide will be sent 2 weeks before the race.
What are the mandatory gear requirements?
Required: 1.5L minimum hydration capacity, emergency whistle, personal cup (no cups at aid stations), headlamp with spare batteries (50M), space blanket. Full gear list provided upon registration.
Is this race ITRA certified?
Yes! The 50K earns 3 ITRA points and the 50 Mile earns 4 ITRA points. Both distances can be used as qualifiers for UTMB and other major international ultras.
What's the refund/deferral policy?
No refunds, but free deferrals to the following year are available until July 1st. After July 1st, deferrals incur a $25 fee. Bib transfers to another runner are available for $35.
