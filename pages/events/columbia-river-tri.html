üåä Frequently Asked Questions

What are the exact distances for each race?
Sprint: 750m swim, 20K bike, 5K run. Olympic: 1.5K swim, 40K bike, 10K run. Half (70.3): 1.9K swim, 90K bike, 21.1K run.
What is the water temperature in August?
The Columbia River typically ranges from 68-72¬∞F (20-22¬∞C) in early August. Wetsuits are allowed but not required at these temperatures. We recommend checking USAT wetsuit guidelines for your category.
Is the swim current-assisted?
Yes! The swim is designed to take advantage of the river's natural current, making for fast times. The course is point-to-point downstream.
What is the refund/transfer policy?
We offer free transfers up to 30 days before the event. Deferrals to the following year are available up to 14 days before. No refunds, but transfers are easy through your registration account.
Are there age group awards?
Yes! We award the top 3 in each 5-year age group for all distances, plus overall male/female winners. Awards ceremony begins at noon on race day.
Is this race USAT sanctioned?
Yes, this is a USAT-sanctioned event. Annual USAT members can race with their membership. One-day licenses are available during registration for non-members.
