Frequently Asked Questions

What are the time cutoffs?
50K: 10 hours | 100K: 20 hours | 100 Mile: 30 hours. Intermediate cutoffs are enforced at major aid stations. Full cutoff schedule provided in your runner's packet.
What's at the aid stations?
Aid stations are stocked with water, electrolytes, cola, ginger ale, fruit, PB&J, quesadillas, soup, broth, chips, candy, and more. Hot food available at major stations. You may also have a drop bag at designated locations.
Can I have a crew and pacer?
Yes! Crew access is available at 4 locations throughout the course. Pacers are allowed for 100K (after mile 40) and 100 Mile (after mile 50) runners. Pacers must check in at the designated aid station.
What's the required gear?
Required: Handheld/hydration pack, headlamp with backup batteries, emergency whistle, and space blanket. Recommended: Poles, extra layers, rain jacket. Full gear list provided upon registration.
Is the course marked?
The course is fully marked with ribbons, signs, and glow sticks (for night sections). GPX files are provided. Course marshals are stationed at key intersections. We have a 98% on-course success rate!
What's the refund/deferral policy?
Deferrals to the following year available until March 15 ($25 fee). Transfers to another runner allowed until March 28. No refunds, but we offer rollover credit for injury with doctor's note.
