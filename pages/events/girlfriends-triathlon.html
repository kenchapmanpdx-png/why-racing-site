üíú Frequently Asked Questions

I've never done a triathlon before. Is this event for me?
Absolutely! The Girlfriends Triathlon is specifically designed to be welcoming for first-timers. We offer a free first-timer clinic, course preview, and our Super Sprint distance is perfect for beginners. Plus, 65% of our participants are doing their first triathlon ‚Äì you'll be surrounded by women just like you!
What are the distances?
Super Sprint: 250m swim, 10K bike, 2.5K run. Sprint: 500m swim, 20K bike, 5K run. Both distances are beginner-friendly and achievable with just a few weeks of training!
Can I sign up with my friends as a group?
Yes! We offer group discounts for 4+ women registering together. Just use code "GIRLFRIENDS" at checkout to save 10%. You'll also be placed in the same wave so you can start together!
What if I'm a slow swimmer?
There is no time limit for the swim! We have lifeguards, kayakers, and paddle boarders throughout the course. You can even hold onto a paddleboard to rest if needed. We want everyone to finish feeling amazing!
Is this event only for women?
The Girlfriends Triathlon is designed as a women-only racing event to create a supportive, empowering environment. However, all genders are welcome to come spectate and cheer!
