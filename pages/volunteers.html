Be Part of the Spirit.

Our volunteers are the heartbeat of every Why Racing event. Join our crew, support amazing athletes, and experience the energy of race day from the inside.

Join the Community

Meet passionate people and make lifelong friends while supporting the local PNW racing scene.

Earn Perks

Get a limited edition volunteer shirt, snacks, and "crew credit" towards future race entries.

Make an Impact

Your encouragement at a water station or course turn can be the difference for an athlete's big day.

Volunteer Roles

From the finish line to the far reaches of the course, we have a spot for you.

Course Marshal

Keep athletes safe and on track at key intersections. High energy and loud cheering required!

Registration

Be the first friendly face athletes see. Help with bib pickup and gear bags.

Aid Stations

The literal life-savers. Hand out water, electrolytes, and endless motivation.

Finish Line Crew

Place medals around necks and celebrate the incredible achievements of every finisher.
