A Highly Desirable Audience

Our athletes aren't just active—they're affluent, educated decision-makers with strong brand loyalty. Runners and triathletes average $112,000 in household income and are 79% college-educated, making them among the most valuable demographics in event marketing.

More importantly: runner identity directly predicts purchase intention.

When athletes connect with brands that support their events, they don't just notice—they buy, they advocate, and they stay loyal for years.

Who You'll Reach

Our participants are committed consumers who invest heavily in performance, recovery, and lifestyle:

Health & Recovery

Massage, physical therapy, chiropractic, sports medicine, coaching. They push hard and pay for expert recovery.

Gear & Equipment

Premium bikes, quality running shoes, technical apparel, GPS devices. Once they find what works, they become brand ambassadors.

Nutrition & Fuel

Energy products, supplements, performance foods. Athletes who find their formula stick with it—and tell everyone.

Lifestyle & Experiences

Restaurants, travel, vehicles, craft beverages, financial services. They work hard, play hard, and spend accordingly.
